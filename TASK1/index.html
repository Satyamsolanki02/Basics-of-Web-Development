📄 HTML Structure

HTML provides the skeleton of every webpage. It uses headings, paragraphs, images, and links to organize content.

Why it matters:

Clean HTML improves accessibility, SEO, and makes your site easier to maintain.

  • Headings (h1 to h6) create a clear outline
  • Paragraphs hold text content
  • Images & Links add media and navigation
